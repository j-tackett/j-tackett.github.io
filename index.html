Little Rock

I was born and raised in the Little Rock Metropolitan area. My first jobs were in food service and retail. I managed the local baseball field concessions stand until I graduated high school, then became a store associate at Cherry's Hallmark for summer work.

Growing up in a small town afforded me a very comfortable living, but from a young age, I jumped at any opportunities to travel. Summer vacations were one of the best ways to explore.

Ecuador

My first opportunity to leave the country came when I was a junior in High School. My local church was taking a group to Ecuador to build a house, lead classes, and otherwise help around an orphanage there. I fell in love with travel then. While many of my companions groaned about "Why is this not in english," or "I don't know how I'm going to survive without xx," I enjoyed getting to try on this new self.
